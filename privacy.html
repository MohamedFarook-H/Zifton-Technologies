Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

1. Information We Collect

At Zifton Technologies, we collect information to provide better hyper-local delivery and software services to all our users. This includes basic account info and precise location data required to process direct market-to-home deliveries via our app.

2. How We Use Information

We use the data we collect to operate, maintain, and improve our ecosystem. Location data is strictly used by Zifton Delivery partners to map the shortest and greenest route from the local market to your doorstep.

3. No-Hub Cancellation Policy (Delivery)

Because we operate a direct-concierge model without dark stores, perishable goods (Food, Meat, Flowers) cannot be canceled once our delivery partner has picked them up from the vendor. Refunds are not applicable for active perishable orders.

4. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding data usage or Zifton Labs, please contact us at support@zifton.com.
